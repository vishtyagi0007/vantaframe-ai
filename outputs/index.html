FAQs

Before you book.

Is this different from wedding photography?

Yes. Your photographer creates polished photo memories. We focus on phone-first BTS clips, vertical reels, trends, candid moments, and fast social delivery.

How fast do we get content?

Most packages include a 24 hour teaser or same-day edit, followed by edited reels and a raw BTS folder based on your package.

Do you work with our photographer and planner?

Yes. We coordinate around timelines, key rituals, and vendor boundaries so coverage feels smooth and respectful.

Can you match our wedding aesthetic?

Yes. We discuss mood, outfits, decor, color palette, preferred audio, and editing style before the wedding.
